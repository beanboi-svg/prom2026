An official website of the Frederick Student Council. Commissioned by the Prom 2026 Planning Committee.

11th and 12th Grade Students & Families,

Prom 2026 will take place on Saturday, April 18th from 8:00-11:00 PM at Mile High Station in Denver (2027 W Colfax Ave, Denver, CO 80204). Parking on-site is included with ticket purchase.

Frederick High School juniors and seniors are permitted to attend Prom. 9th and 10th grade FHS students, as well as students from other high schools, may attend with a completed and approved guest form. Guest forms must include signatures from the guest's parent/guardian and school administrator, along with a photocopy of the guest's ID (school-issued or government-issued). All guests must be of high school age, with the only exception being Frederick High School Class of 2025 graduates with an approved guest form.

FHS students must also obtain an approval signature from an FHS administrator prior to submitting their form to Ms. Precht in the counseling office. Guest forms are available in the main office. All guest forms are due by Thursday, April 16th. Students are encouraged to pick up forms and begin the approval process as soon as possible.

Prom tickets are $50 per person. Ticket sales will begin on Monday, March 30th and will be available both online and in person during first and second lunch. Ticket sales will close on Thursday, April 16th, and tickets will not be sold at the door. The online ticket link will be shared in the Golden Feather weekly newsletter and on this year's Prom website: prom26.com

After Prom will take place at Frederick High School from 11:00 PM-2:30 AM, sponsored by the FHS Booster Club. This free event includes food, games, and prizes. All FHS juniors and seniors are encouraged to attend, and approved Prom guests are also welcome. Please note that for After Prom, guests (non FHS 11th and 12th grade students) need to arrive and enter After Prom at the same time with the student who completed a guest form on their behalf.

Students in need of formal wear for Prom 2026 may contact Ms. Precht at precht_madison@svvsd.org to schedule a time to browse gently used or new donated items through Student Council's Project Prom initiative. Student Council is also accepting donations of new or gently used formal wear in the main office before and after Prom.

If you have any questions, please contact Maddie Precht, Student Council Sponsor, at precht_madison@svvsd.org or 303-833-3533.

We look forward to providing a fun, memorable, and safe Prom experience for all students.
